hieroglyph_addons¶

Patches the hard-coded Heroglyph slides theme.

Warning

These modifications only in Heroglyph slides builds.

Note

extension is automatically loaded when added to extensions, no directives or configurations required.

  1. Make quotations small enough to fit on the slide.

    A quotation should not overwhelm the entire slide, it should fit in neatly

    – Me

  1. Add a bit of space between slide titles and images.

  1. Don’t display glossary terms as hyperlinks.

  1. Make code-block captions legible.
